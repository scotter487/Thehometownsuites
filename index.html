The Borough

Everyone's Hometown

Media, Pennsylvania is a walkable, vibrant small town — the county seat of Delaware County — with award-winning dining, independent shops, live music, and the last downtown trolley in suburban America.

Photos courtesy of Media, PA

🍻 Award-Winning Dining

Dozens of independent restaurants line State Street, from farm-to-table to global cuisine.

🚗 America's Last Downtown Trolley

SEPTA Route 101 runs straight down State Street — a beloved piece of living history.

🏭 Boutique Shopping

Independent shops, galleries, and markets make State Street a true Main Street destination.

🎶 Year-Round Events

Street fairs, outdoor dining nights, art shows, and live music bring the borough to life.
